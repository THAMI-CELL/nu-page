Welcome

Since I Began In 2019

My journey in web development began in 2018. I always had the love and passion for computers but it then grew big when was curious of how I can develop websites and mobile apps using my laptop.

On the year 2019, after a full year of learning of how to code, I felt confident about my coding skills. On the 9th of September 2019 I began my career as a freelancer. I developed simple static websites, logos and mock-ups for free to improve my skills.

Currently, Thami's Code Gallery will be expanding into Mobile App Development. Very soon Thami's Code Gallery will also be Launching a Live Internet Radio, supporting local artists.
